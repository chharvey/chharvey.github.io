  1. Marcus was told to simplify the expression \left(1+5\right)\cdot2. In his calculator, he pressed 1 + 5 × 2 = and his answer was 11. Is this correct? If not, what did he do wrong?

    No, he didn’t perform the operations in the correct order. He should have used parentheses, or done 1 + 5 = and got 6, and then 6 × 2 = and got 12.

  2. Olive was told to simplify the expression \frac{15-3}{4}. In her calculator, she pressed 1 5 − 3 ÷ 4 = and her answer was 14.25. Is this correct? If not, what did she do wrong?

    No, she didn’t perform the operations in the correct order. She should have used parentheses, or done 1 5 − 3 = and got 12, and then 1 2 ÷ 4 = and got 3.
